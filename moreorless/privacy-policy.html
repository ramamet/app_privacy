Privacy Policy

More Or Less: Calorie Game

Last Updated: December 2025

Introduction

Welcome to More Or Less ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how our app works. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application "More Or Less: Calorie Game" (the "App").

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is our priority.

Information We Collect

More Or Less is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information from you.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • Game Progress: High scores, achievement progress, games played
  • Preferences: Sound settings, difficulty preferences
  • Premium Status: Whether you have purchased the premium upgrade
  • Daily Play Count: Number of plays remaining (free users)
Data Type Collected? Shared?
Personal Information (name, email) No No

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob (Free Version Only)

The free version of our App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect certain information to serve personalized ads. This is handled entirely by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Premium users do not see any advertisements, and AdMob is not active for premium accounts.

Google Play Billing

In-app purchases are processed through Google Play. Payment information is handled entirely by Google and is never shared with us. We only receive confirmation of purchase status (purchased or not purchased).

Children's Privacy

Our App is not specifically targeted at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since our App does not collect personal information from any users, we are compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements.

Data Security

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it. We do not maintain any servers that store user data. Your data security depends on your device's security settings.

How to Delete Your Data

  • Go to your device Settings
  • Navigate to Apps > More Or Less
  • Select "Clear Data" or "Clear Storage"
  • Alternatively, uninstall the App to remove all data

Offline Functionality

More Or Less is designed to work completely offline. Once installed, no internet connection is required to play the game. Internet is only used for:

  • Loading advertisements (free version only)
  • Processing in-app purchases

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, traditional data rights (access, correction, deletion) do not apply in the conventional sense. However, you always have the right to:

  • Delete local data: Clear app data through your device settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads: Adjust your device's ad settings
  • Remove the App: Uninstall at any time, which removes all local data
  • Purchase Premium: Remove all advertisements from the App

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Continued use of the App after any modifications indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
