Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026  ·  App: MyBuildCost (com.mybuildcost.mybuildcost)

MyBuildCost is a house-construction expense tracker. Your data stays on your device. We do not collect it, we cannot see it, and it is never sent to us or to any third-party server by the app.

The short version
  • All your expenses, people, budgets and photos are stored only on your phone.
  • MyBuildCost has no account, no sign-in, and no cloud sync.
  • We (the developer) do not collect, receive, or store any of your data.
  • The only network feature is in-app purchase, handled entirely by Google Play.

1. Information you enter

MyBuildCost lets you record information to manage your construction spending, such as:

  • Expense amounts, dates, notes, categories, tags and construction phases;
  • Receiver details you choose to add (name, and optionally a phone number or UPI ID);
  • Budgets, milestones and rate-card figures;
  • Receipt or progress photos you optionally attach.

This information is saved in a private database on your device. It is not transmitted to us or to anyone else. We have no access to it.

2. Information we collect

None. MyBuildCost does not collect analytics, usage statistics, advertising identifiers, location, or any personal or financial data. The app contains no advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking software.

3. Device permissions

MyBuildCost only requests permissions when you use the related feature:

  • Camera / Photos — only if you choose to attach a receipt or progress photo. Selected images are stored locally with your expense. The app does not upload them.

4. Purchases (MyBuildCost Pro)

The optional one-time MyBuildCost Pro upgrade is sold through Google Play Billing. Your payment is processed entirely by Google — the app never sees or stores your payment-card or billing details. Google’s handling of that transaction is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. The app only stores, locally, a simple flag recording whether Pro has been unlocked.

5. Backup, export & sharing

You can export your data to a backup file (JSON) from within the app. This file is created on your device. If you then choose to share that file — or a report — the data goes only to the destination you pick (for example another app, email, or storage location) through your device’s standard share sheet. MyBuildCost does not send your backups anywhere on its own, and there is no automatic cloud backup.

6. Data security

Because your data never leaves your device through the app, it is protected by your device’s own security (screen lock, encryption, and app sandboxing). We recommend keeping a device lock enabled and storing any exported backup files somewhere safe, as those files contain your records in readable form.

7. Data retention & deletion

Your data remains on your device until you remove it. You can:

  • Delete individual expenses, receivers, budgets or milestones within the app;
  • Use Clear All Data in the app to erase everything; or
  • Uninstall the app, which removes its local database and attached photos.

Since we never receive your data, there is nothing for us to delete on your behalf.

8. Children’s privacy

MyBuildCost is a general-purpose finance/utility tool and is not directed at children. It does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

9. Third-party services

The only third party involved is Google Play (for app distribution and, if you buy Pro, billing). No other third-party services, SDKs, ad networks, or analytics providers are used.

10. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above and post the revised policy at this URL. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.
