MedTaken Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Summary: MedTaken stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

Introduction

MedTaken ("the App") is a medication tracking application that helps you record when you take your medications using photo proof. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect any personal data. All information you enter into the App remains on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device:

  • Medication names and schedules you create
  • Photos you take as medication proof
  • Dose records and timestamps
  • App settings and preferences

Data NOT Collected

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device information
  • Any health data beyond what you enter

Camera Permission

The App requests camera access to capture medication proof photos. These photos are:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never uploaded to any server
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Accessible only within the App

Data Security

Your data is stored in the App's private storage area on your device. This storage is:

  • Accessible only by the MedTaken app
  • Protected by Android's security model
  • Not backed up to cloud services by default

Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete individual medications at any time
  • Delete all data via Settings > Delete All Data
  • Uninstalling the App removes all stored data

Third-Party Services

MedTaken does not use any third-party services that collect data, including:

  • No analytics services
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media integrations

Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
