MomentKeeper Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 July 2026

MomentKeeper is a calm, offline daily check-in tracker. It is built so that everything you record stays on your device. This policy explains, plainly, what that means.

The short version

  • MomentKeeper does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
  • There is no account and no sign-up.
  • There are no ads, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.
  • The app makes no network calls of its own. Its only network use is Google Play handling the optional purchase.

What the app stores, and where

Your habits, your daily check-ins, and your settings are stored only in MomentKeeper's private storage on your device. They are never sent anywhere. They leave your device only if you back up your device yourself through your own tools, or when you uninstall the app, at which point they are removed.

What the app does not collect

MomentKeeper does not collect names, email addresses, contacts, location, photos, device identifiers, usage analytics, or any other personal information. The app's own code makes no network requests. The only network use is Google Play handling the optional purchase, described below.

The optional one-time purchase

MomentKeeper offers an optional one-time in-app purchase, MomentKeeper Plus. If you choose to buy it, the purchase is handled entirely by Google Play. MomentKeeper never sees your payment details. The app stores only a single private flag on your device recording that Plus was unlocked. Google's handling of the transaction is covered by Google's Privacy Policy.

Notifications

If you turn on the optional daily reminder, MomentKeeper schedules a local notification on your device. This uses no network and sends nothing off the device. You can turn it off at any time in the app or in your system settings.

Children

MomentKeeper does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children. There is nothing to collect.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this same address with a new date above. Any change that would affect data handling would be described here before it took effect.
