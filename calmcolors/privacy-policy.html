1. Introduction

Welcome to Calm Colors - Sort & Relax ("we," "our," or "the app"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

This Privacy Policy explains how Calm Colors handles (or more accurately, doesn't handle) your information. By using our app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Information We DO NOT Collect

Calm Colors is designed with privacy at its core. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store:

  • Personal Information: No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact details
  • Location Data: We don't track where you are or where you've been
  • Usage Analytics: We don't monitor how you use the app, which palettes you prefer, or how long you play
  • Device Information: No device IDs, advertising IDs, or hardware specifications
  • Crash Reports: We don't automatically collect crash logs (though Google Play may collect anonymized crash data)
  • Behavioral Data: No tracking of taps, swipes, or interaction patterns
  • Biometric Data: No fingerprints, face scans, or health information
Why? We believe therapeutic apps should be sanctuaries, not surveillance tools. You deserve screen time that truly respects your privacy.

4. In-App Purchases

4.1 How Purchases Work

Calm Colors offers a PRO unlock as a one-time in-app purchase. All purchase transactions are processed entirely by Google Play, not by us.

4.2 What Google Play Collects

When you make a purchase, Google Play may collect:

  • Your Google account information
  • Payment method details (credit card, PayPal, etc.)
  • Purchase history and receipts
  • Transaction metadata (date, amount, product ID)

We do NOT have access to your payment information. Google's privacy policy governs purchase data: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.3 What We Receive

We only receive a purchase receipt token from Google Play to verify that you own the PRO unlock. This token:

  • Cannot identify you personally
  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is used solely to unlock PRO features

5. Third-Party Services

Calm Colors does NOT integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No third parties receive data from this app.

The only third-party interaction is with Google Play for:

  • App distribution and updates
  • In-app purchase processing
  • Optional anonymized crash reporting (controlled by Google Play, not us)

6. Internet Connectivity

Calm Colors works 100% offline. The app does not require an internet connection to function.

Internet access is only used for:

  • Initial app download from Google Play (one-time)
  • App updates when available (optional)
  • In-app purchase verification through Google Play (only when purchasing/restoring PRO)

After installation, you can use Calm Colors in airplane mode without any limitations.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Calm Colors is rated Everyone and is safe for all ages, including children under 13. Since we collect zero personal information, we are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Parents can be confident that:

  • No personal data is collected from children (or anyone)
  • There are no social features, chat, or user-generated content
  • In-app purchases require parental authorization through Google Play's family controls

8. International Users & GDPR Compliance

Calm Colors is available worldwide. Since we do not collect or process any personal data, we are compliant with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and similar privacy laws globally.

Your GDPR Rights (Even Though We Don't Have Your Data)

9. Data Security

While we don't collect data, we still take security seriously:
