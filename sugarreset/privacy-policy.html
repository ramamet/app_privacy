1Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Health Data (Stored Locally Only)

SugarReset stores the following information exclusively on your device. This data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly export it:

  • Daily Sugar Logs: Sugar intake amounts (in grams), timestamps, and optional notes
  • Food Presets: Custom food items you create for quick logging
  • Challenge Data: Active and completed sugar reduction challenges
  • Progress Metrics: Streaks, achievements, XP levels, daily quests
  • Settings: Daily goals, theme preferences, notification settings
  • Craving Context (Premium): Optional mood, location type, and trigger information

Important: All this data is stored using local device storage (Hive database). We have zero access to this information. Even if we wanted to see it, we technically cannot.

1.2 Data We Never Collect

To be crystal clear, SugarReset does NOT collect:

  • ❌ Name, email, phone number, or any identifying information
  • ❌ Precise location data or GPS coordinates
  • ❌ Contacts, photos, or other media files
  • ❌ Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, etc.) for tracking
  • ❌ Browsing history or app usage patterns
  • ❌ Microphone, camera, or sensor data
  • ❌ Health data from other apps or fitness trackers

1.3 Anonymous Technical Data (Opt-In Only)

If you explicitly consent in the app settings, we may collect minimal anonymous crash reports via Sentry to improve app stability:

  • Device type and Android version (e.g., "Pixel 7, Android 14")
  • App version number
  • Crash logs and error messages (sanitized, no personal data)
  • Anonymous session ID (cannot be linked to you)

Default Setting: Crash reporting is OFF by default. You must opt-in manually.

2How We Use Your Information

2.1 On-Device Processing

All your health data is processed locally on your device to:

  • Calculate daily sugar intake and show progress rings
  • Track streaks and determine if you've met your goals
  • Generate calendar heatmaps and statistics charts
  • Award XP, unlock achievements, and complete quests
  • Provide insights like "sugar cubes saved" and trend analysis
  • Send local notifications (reminders, achievement unlocks)

No cloud processing. Your device does all the work.

2.2 Crash Reports (If Opted In)

If you enable crash reporting, we use anonymous data to:

  • Identify and fix bugs that cause app crashes
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Test compatibility with different devices

This data is processed by Sentry (see Section 4.2) and deleted after 90 days.

3Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All your data is stored in an encrypted local database (Hive) on your Android device. This data:

  • Resides in your app's private storage directory
  • Is protected by Android's app sandboxing security
  • Cannot be accessed by other apps (without root access)
  • Is automatically deleted if you uninstall the app

3.2 Backup & Export

Premium users can create manual backups:

  • JSON Backup: Exports all data to a file saved in your device's Downloads folder
  • CSV Export: Exports logs as a spreadsheet for external analysis

Your responsibility: Backup files are saved to your device storage. If you share or upload them anywhere (cloud storage, email, etc.), that's outside our control. Keep backups secure.

3.3 Data Retention

When you purchase SugarReset Premium, we use RevenueCat to process the transaction. RevenueCat may collect:

Purpose: Improve app stability and fix bugs

SugarReset does NOT use:

  • ❌ Analytics platforms (no Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)

  • ❌ Cloud storage providers (no automatic backups to cloud)
  • ❌ AI/ML services (all calculations done on-device)

5Your Privacy Rights

5.1 GDPR Rights (European Users)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data (use in-app export feature)
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data (edit logs directly in app)
  • Right to Erasure: Delete all your data (Settings → Delete All Data)
  • Right to Restriction: Limit processing (disable crash reporting)
  • Right to Data Portability: Export data in CSV/JSON format (Premium feature)
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of crash reporting anytime

5.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect (see Section 1)
  • Delete personal information we hold (use in-app deletion)
  • Opt-out of sale of personal data (We don't sell your data. Ever.)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

5.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  1. Access your data: Open the app, view all logs, settings, and progress
  2. Export your data: Settings → Backup & Export → Export to CSV/JSON (Premium)
  3. Delete your data: Settings → Data Management → Delete All Data
  4. Opt-out of crash reports: Settings → Privacy → Crash Reporting → OFF

For questions or assistance, contact us at privacy@sugarreset.app

6Children's Privacy

6.1 Age Restrictions

SugarReset is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Since we don't collect personal data at all, and the app is used offline without account creation, there's minimal privacy risk for younger users.

6.2 Parental Consent

If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has used SugarReset and wants to delete the data, simply uninstall the app from the child's device. All data will be permanently deleted.
