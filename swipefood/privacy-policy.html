Privacy Policy

SwipeFood - Swipe. Guess. Learn.
Last updated: December 23, 2025

SwipeFood ("we", "our", or "us") operates the SwipeFood mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Summary: SwipeFood does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All game data is stored locally on your device.

Information Collection and Use

SwipeFood is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information from users. The App does not require:

  • Account creation or registration
  • Email address or phone number
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Device identifiers for tracking

Local Data Storage

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Game Progress: Your scores, streaks, and statistics
  • Settings: Sound preferences, game mode selections
  • Premium Status: Whether you have purchased premium features

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

In-App Purchases

SwipeFood offers optional in-app purchases to unlock premium features. These purchases are processed entirely through Google Play Store. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or billing address.

Google Play handles all payment processing according to their own privacy policy. You can review Google's privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing: For processing in-app purchases only

We do not use any analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking tools.

Children's Privacy

SwipeFood is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy. The App:

  • Does not collect personal information from anyone, including children
  • Does not contain any social features or user-to-user communication
  • Does not display advertisements
  • Does not link to external websites or social media

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect or transmit any personal information, your data security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Using a secure lock screen
  • Only downloading apps from official app stores

Permissions

SwipeFood requests the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required for processing in-app purchases through Google Play
  • Billing: Required to verify and restore purchases

The App does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive permissions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information for us to provide, modify, or delete. However, you can:

  • Clear all App data through your device settings
  • Uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data
  • Request restoration of your purchases through Google Play

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the App, please contact us:

  • Email: support@swipefood.app
  • App: SwipeFood on Google Play Store
