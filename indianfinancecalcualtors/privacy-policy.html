How the App Works

India Finance Calculators performs all calculations locally on your device. Here's what this means:

  • Offline Processing: All EMI, SIP, FD, and other calculations are performed directly on your device without any server communication.
  • No Data Transmission: The numbers you enter (loan amounts, interest rates, etc.) never leave your phone.
  • Local Storage Only: Your theme preference (light/dark mode) is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to us.

Data Storage

The only data stored by the app is your theme preference (light or dark mode), which is saved locally on your device using standard Android storage mechanisms. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing app data

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services including:
