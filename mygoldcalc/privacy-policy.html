Privacy Policy for MyGoldCalc

Last Updated: December 2025

Introduction

MyGoldCalc ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our gold calculator app.

In short: We don't collect personal data. Your calculations stay on your device. No account required.

Information We Collect

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal identification (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Financial information
  • Payment details (handled securely by Google Play)

Information We May Collect

  • Anonymous Usage Data: App feature usage, crash reports, and performance metrics through Firebase Analytics
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, and app version for compatibility purposes
  • Cached Data: Gold rates are cached locally on your device for offline functionality

How We Use Information

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Understand which features are most useful
  • No data is sold or shared with third parties for marketing

Data Storage

  • All calculation data stays on your device
  • Gold rate cache stored locally
  • No cloud storage of personal data
  • Settings stored locally using device storage

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and in-app purchases
  • Supabase: To fetch latest gold rates (no personal data is sent or stored)
  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (optional)
  • Google AdMob: Advertising in free version only

These services have their own privacy policies that govern their data collection practices.

Children's Privacy

MyGoldCalc does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is rated for Everyone and contains no age-restricted content.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Clear app data anytime from your device settings
  • Opt-out of analytics through device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@mygoldcalc.app

This app is designed with privacy in mind. We believe you shouldn't need to share personal data to calculate gold prices.
