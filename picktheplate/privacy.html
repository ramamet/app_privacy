Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

App Name: Pick the Plate : Food Quiz

Package: com.picktheplate.pick_the_plate

Overview

Pick the Plate : Food Quiz ("the App") is a calorie guessing game that helps users learn about nutrition through fun gameplay. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Key Points:
  • We do NOT collect personal information
  • We do NOT require account registration
  • All game data is stored locally on your device
  • The App works completely offline

Information We Collect

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Photos or media from your device
  • Financial information (payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play)
  • Device identifiers for advertising
  • Browsing history
  • Any user-generated content

Information Stored Locally

The App stores the following data only on your device:

Data Type Purpose Storage
Game scores Track high scores and streaks Device only
Game statistics Show gameplay analytics Device only
Settings preferences Remember sound/vibration settings Device only
Premium status Verify purchase status Device only
Achievement progress Track unlocked achievements Device only

This data never leaves your device, is not transmitted to any server, is not shared with any third party, and can be deleted by uninstalling the App or clearing app data.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases processed through Google Play Store.

How Purchases Work

  • All payment processing is handled by Google Play
  • We never see or store your payment information
  • Google Play handles all transaction security
  • Purchase verification happens through Google Play's secure systems

For Google Play's privacy practices, see: Google Play Terms of Service

Third-Party Services

Google Play Services

The App uses Google Play Services for in-app purchase processing and app distribution. Google Play Services may collect certain device information as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics services. We do not track app usage patterns, user behavior, session duration, feature usage, or crash reports.

Advertising

This App does not display advertisements. We do not show ads, use advertising SDKs, share data with advertising networks, or track users for advertising purposes.

Children's Privacy

Pick the Plate is suitable for users of all ages and is rated "Everyone" on Google Play.

  • We do not knowingly collect information from children
  • No account creation is required
  • No social features or communication tools exist
  • No in-app purchases can be made without device owner authorization

Parents can manage in-app purchase permissions through their device's parental control settings.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, security measures focus on:

  • Local Storage: Game data is stored using secure local storage mechanisms
  • Purchase Verification: All purchase verification uses Google Play's secure APIs
  • No Network Transmission: Game data never leaves your device

Data Retention and Deletion

Your Data, Your Control

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • You can delete all data by uninstalling the App or clearing app data in device settings

What Happens When You Uninstall

When you uninstall the App, all local game data is permanently deleted. Your purchase history remains with Google Play and can be restored if you reinstall.

Your Rights

Even though we don't collect personal data, you have the right to:

  • Access: Know what data is stored (only local game progress)
  • Delete: Remove all data by uninstalling or clearing app data
  • Portability: Your game data is stored locally only
  • Opt-out: No tracking to opt out of

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
