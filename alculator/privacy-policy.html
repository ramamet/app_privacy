Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 17, 2025

Our Commitment: Alculator is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your drinking data stays on YOUR device. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal drinking information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Alculator ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application Alculator - BAC Calculator (the "App").

By using Alculator, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use Alculator, you may provide:

  • Profile Information: Gender, weight, and home state (used for BAC calculations and displaying relevant state laws)
  • Drinking Session Data: Drinks consumed, times, and calculated BAC values
  • Preferences: Theme settings, notification preferences, and app configuration
Important: All of this information is stored LOCALLY on your device only. We do not have access to your drinking data, and it is never transmitted to our servers.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Data Type Purpose Stored Where
Approximate Location Auto-detect your state for displaying relevant DUI laws Device only (not transmitted)
Purchase Information Verify premium subscription status via Google Play Google Play (their privacy policy applies)

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name or contact information
  • Your exact/precise location
  • Your drinking history (stored locally only)
  • Device identifiers for tracking
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Any data for advertising purposes

3. How We Use Information

Information is used solely to provide App functionality:

  • BAC Calculation: Your weight and gender are used locally to calculate estimated blood alcohol content
  • State Law Display: Your location or selected state determines which DUI laws to display
  • Premium Features: Purchase status determines feature access
  • App Preferences: Settings are stored to personalize your experience

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All personal data (profile, drinking sessions, preferences) is stored exclusively on your device using encrypted local storage. This data is:

  • Never transmitted to external servers
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Deleted when you uninstall the app or clear app data

4.2 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your locally stored data, including encryption of sensitive information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

Alculator uses the following third-party services:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Google Play Billing Process premium purchases Purchase transactions (managed by Google)
Google Play Services App distribution and updates Standard app metrics

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review: Google Privacy Policy

6. Location Data

Alculator requests location permission solely to automatically detect your state for displaying relevant DUI laws. This location data is:

  • Used only for state detection (approximate location)
  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Never transmitted to our servers
  • Never stored beyond the immediate use

You can deny location permission and manually select your state instead.

7. Children's Privacy

Alculator is intended for users who are of legal drinking age (21+ in the United States). We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 21 years of age. If you are under 21, please do not use this App.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored on your device and accessible to you
  • Deletion: Clear app data or uninstall the app to delete all stored information
  • Location: Deny or revoke location permission in your device settings
  • Notifications: Disable notifications in app settings or device settings

9. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data persists until you clear app data or uninstall the app
  • We have no ability to retain or delete your data remotely
  • Session history is stored according to your subscription level (5 sessions for free, unlimited for premium)

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights regarding your personal information. Because Alculator stores all data locally on your device and does not collect or sell personal information, most CCPA provisions do not apply. However, you always have the right to delete your data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

12. International Users

Alculator is designed for use in the United States. State DUI laws and BAC limits are specific to US jurisdictions. If you use the app outside the United States, please be aware that laws in your jurisdiction may differ.

Disclaimer: Alculator provides ESTIMATES only. Individual BAC results vary significantly based on metabolism, health, food intake, medications, and other factors. This app is NOT a substitute for a breathalyzer or blood test. NEVER rely solely on this app to determine if you are safe to drive.
