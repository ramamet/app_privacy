1. Introduction

Welcome to CaffeineReset ("App", "we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

CaffeineReset is a caffeine tracking and reduction app that helps users monitor their caffeine intake, set reduction goals, and improve their sleep quality through better caffeine management.

By using CaffeineReset, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

CaffeineReset stores the following information only on your device:

  • Caffeine Intake Logs: Records of drinks you log, including type, amount, caffeine content, and timestamp
  • User Preferences: Daily caffeine limit, bedtime settings, notification preferences
  • Profile Information: Metabolism factors (smoking status, medication use), sensitivity quiz results
  • Challenge Progress: Active challenges, completion status, streak data
  • Achievement Data: Unlocked achievements and badges
  • Custom Drinks: Any custom drinks you create
  • Withdrawal Logs: Symptom tracking data if you use the withdrawal support feature

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be absolutely clear about what we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Any data transmitted to external servers
  • Photos, contacts, or other device data

3. How We Use Information

All data processing happens entirely on your device. The information you enter is used to:

  • Calculate your current caffeine levels using pharmacokinetic models
  • Display personalized predictions (bedtime caffeine levels, metabolism curves)
  • Track your progress toward daily limits and reduction goals
  • Provide achievement notifications and streak tracking
  • Generate insights and analytics about your caffeine habits

No data leaves your device. We do not have access to any of your information.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by the operating system. We use Hive, an encrypted local database, to store your information.

4.2 Data Security

Since all data remains on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security features (PIN, fingerprint, face unlock)
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • There are no servers that could be breached
  • Your information cannot be accessed by us or any third party

4.3 Data Backup

Your app data may be included in your device's standard backup (Google Backup, etc.) if you have this feature enabled on your device. This is controlled by your device settings, not by our app.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties.

CaffeineReset:

  • Does not contain advertisements
  • Does not include analytics SDKs
  • Does not integrate with social media platforms
  • Does not sell, rent, or trade any user information
  • Does not transmit data to any external servers

6. Third-Party Services

CaffeineReset does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The app functions entirely offline without requiring internet connectivity.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access Your Data

All your data is visible within the app. You can view your intake history, settings, and progress at any time.

7.2 Export Your Data

You can export all your data in JSON format through the app's Settings menu. This creates a local file on your device that you control.

7.3 Delete Your Data

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual entries: Remove specific intake logs from the history
  • Clear all data: Use the "Clear All Data" option in Settings to permanently delete everything
  • Uninstall the app: Removing the app will delete all associated data from your device

7.4 Modify Your Data

You can edit any intake entry, update your preferences, or modify your profile at any time within the app.

8. Children's Privacy

CaffeineReset is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. The app is designed for adults who consume caffeine and wish to track or reduce their intake.

We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since all data is stored locally and we have no access to user data, we cannot identify or remove children's data. If you believe a child is using this app, please ensure they uninstall it, which will remove all data.

10. Health Information Disclaimer

CaffeineReset provides caffeine tracking and educational information. Please note:

  • The app is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease
  • Caffeine level calculations are estimates based on pharmacokinetic models and may not be accurate for all individuals
  • Consult a healthcare professional before making significant changes to your caffeine intake, especially if you have health conditions
  • The app should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice
