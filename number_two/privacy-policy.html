Welcome to Number Two — Poop Tracker ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob is a third-party advertising service operated by Google LLC.

Google AdMob may collect certain information to serve personalized advertisements, including:

You can control ad personalization through your device settings:

The App allows you to export your data in the following ways:

When you choose to export or share data, you are responsible for how that exported data is used and protected.

Important: Once you export data from the App, we have no control over how it is stored, shared, or protected. Please be mindful when sharing health information.

6. Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • No Account Required: No login credentials to protect
